Japan is renown for its refinement of all societal elements. It seems no small detail escapes purposeful improvement by her responsible citizens of all backgrounds. Yet the timeless treasures of this eastern nation are not confined to humanity's contributions; Japan is also marked by its natural splendor. It hosts an array of beautiful natural environments, including the numerous forested areas that dot the island nation.
